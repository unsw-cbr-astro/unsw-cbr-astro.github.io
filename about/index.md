The astrophysics group is a part of the School of Science @ UNSW Canberra.

## Research Areas

* Astronomy and Astrophysics
* Stellar Astronomy and Planetary Systems
* Galactic Astronomy
* Supernovae
* White dwarf binaries
* Transients
* Nucleosynthesis
